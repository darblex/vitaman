# VITAMAN - Telegram Growth Plan (Execution-Ready)

## Goal
Bring consistent daily orders from Telegram using one clean funnel:
Ads/Channels -> Landing/Bot Deep Link -> Telegram Bot -> Human close.

## Core Funnel
1. User clicks a tracked deep link.
2. User opens bot with campaign source in `start` payload.
3. User selects product and starts checkout quickly.
4. User completes order and moves to representative chat.

## Setup Once (Day 0)
- Open one official channel for trust and authority.
- Set channel bio with one CTA to bot:
  - `https://t.me/DrViagrashop_Bot?start=channel_bio`
- Pin 3 posts:
  - Who we are
  - How ordering works
  - Current top offers
- Keep all traffic Telegram-only (no WhatsApp steps).

## Daily Publishing Plan (30 Days)
- 2 posts per day in channel:
  - 1 value/trust post
  - 1 direct offer post
- 1 story/update post (if available)
- 1 repost/collab in external relevant channels
- Rotate creatives and copy every 3-4 days.

## Weekly Campaign Structure
- 3 source buckets:
  - Paid ads
  - Channel collaborations
  - Organic channel content
- Minimum tracking naming:
  - `ad_a1`, `ad_a2`, `collab_c1`, `channel_p1`
- Keep a simple KPI sheet:
  - clicks -> bot starts -> checkouts -> paid orders

## Content Framework That Converts
- Hook (pain/desire) + proof + single CTA.
- Keep post length short (4-8 lines).
- One CTA only:
  - `https://t.me/DrViagrashop_Bot?start=<source>`

## 5 Offer Angles To Rotate
1. Fast discreet delivery.
2. Human support in Telegram.
3. Quantity savings.
4. Confidence and privacy.
5. Limited-time urgency.

## Optimization Loop (Every 72 Hours)
1. Stop low-performing sources.
2. Duplicate top source with new copy.
3. Keep winning CTA and change only one variable at a time.
4. Track by source payload only (no guesswork).

## Compliance Notes
- Avoid explicit medical claims.
- Avoid unsafe promises.
- Use neutral language around support, privacy, and service speed.
